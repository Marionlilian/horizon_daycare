About Horizon Daycare

At Horizon Daycare, we believe every child deserves a bright beginning. Our center provides a warm, secure environment where children can learn, play, and grow at their own pace.

We combine structured learning with creative play, ensuring each child receives the attention and care they need to thrive. Our experienced staff is dedicated to fostering curiosity, building confidence, and supporting families throughout their childcare journey.

Located in the heart of Regina, we serve families with flexible hours and a commitment to excellence in early childhood education.

Founded by Parents, For Parents

Saima Babir and Rana Waseem, the founders of Horizon Daycare, bring their passion for child development and years of experience to create a daycare that feels like a home away from home.

Their vision is simple: provide exceptional care that gives parents peace of mind and gives children the foundation they need for lifelong success.
